You have reached the end of the manual, but you have not reached the end of the war.

If anything, reading this makes you more of a target, not less. The enemy does not waste ammunition on civilians who are asleep; he targets soldiers who are awake. By picking up these protocols, you have signaled that you are awake. You have moved from being a casualty to being a combatant. The enemy now has a radar lock on you.

## The Definition of Victory

Do not mistake "knowing the protocols" for "being invincible." You will still have bad days. You will still feel anxiety.

You will still get knocked down.

The difference is that now you know how to get back up.

- Before, a bad day became a bad month. Now, it is just a bad morning, because you know how to run The Watchman protocol.
- Before, a crisis paralyzed you. Now, you know how to drop The Anchor.
- Before, you suffered alone. Now, you know how to be a Mat Bearer for someone else.

Victory is not the absence of attacks; victory is the presence of peace during the attack.

## Your Deployment

You are now carrying a set of keys. Do not keep them in your pocket.

Look around you. Your family, your coworkers, and your friends are walking through the same valleys you just learned to navigate. They are drowning in the same storms you just learned to survive.

You are no longer just a survivor; you are a medic. When you see someone hurting, do not just offer them a cliché. Offer them a protocol. Show them the way to the Feet of Jesus.

## The Final Benediction

> "The Lord bless you and keep you; the Lord make his face shine on you and be gracious to you; the Lord turn his face toward you and give you peace." (Numbers 6:24-26)

The manual is closed. The altar is open. You have your orders.
